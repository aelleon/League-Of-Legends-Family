Parents:

Teemo

Teemo is a yordle who reigns from Bandle City. He frequently participates in solo missions in the ongoing defense of Bandle City. His go-to weapon is the blowgun that uses a rare ajunta poison that he personally gathers from the jungles of Kumungu

Tristana

Tristana comes from the region of Bandle City, where she carries her trust cannon Boomer to aid her in Runeterra. Ever since she heard of the adventures had by great warriors, she has strived to become worth of a legend.

Cousins:

Wukong

Wukong rises from the Vastayan tribe in Ionia. He has dedicated his life to preventing Ionia from falling to ruin with his enchanted wooden staff. In this line of work, he met his life long friend, Master Yi, and works together to fend off those who seek to disturb the peace of Ionia.
