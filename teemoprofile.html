Teemo

Biography:

Teemo is a yordle who reigns from Bandle City. He frequently participates in solo missions in the ongoing defense of Bandle City. His go-to weapon is the blowgun that uses a rare ajunta poison that he personally gathers from the jungles of Kumungu!

Born: 02/01/2017

Life Events

Born: 02/01/2017

Teemo was born in Bandle City

Slain: 02/02/2017

Teemo was slain in battle on the Summoning Grounds after a back and forth with Swain. However, Teemo's sacrifice gave his team Baron which was enough to win the overall fight.

Revived: 02/01/2017

Teemo was revived after a 30 second timer.
